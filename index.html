About Us

The Virginia Code Education Conference (VCEC), formerly known as the ‘Joint Conference’, is the training partnership of all VA code industry organizations.

Partnered Organizations

VBCOA

VPMIA

VI IAEI

VFPA

VAESA

WICED of VA

DHCD

JPVBCA

This new name reflects the new permanent partnership, leadership, and focus of conference to provide training relative and of interest for all persons involved in the development, enforcement, and use of the codes.

This conference is held about every three years and is open to all those interested in attending as it is intended to enhance or supplement the training efforts currently offered by the organizations and DHCD.

DHCD & ICC preferred provider continuing education hours will be available and may also be recognized for credit by other industry organizations.

Features of the VCEC include

  • Larger exhibit space - more vendors
  • Wide variety of training or education topics of interest or relevance
  • Open to all who are involved in enforcement, development, or use of the codes
  • Networking activities
