About Us

The Virginia Code Education Conference (VCEC), formerly known as the ‘Joint Conference’, is the training partnership of all Virginia code industry organizations.

Partnered Organizations

VBCOA

VPMIA

VA-IAEI

VFPA

VAESA

WICED of VA

DHCD

JPVBCA

This name reflects the permanent partnership, leadership, and focus of conference to provide training relative and of interest for all persons involved in the development, enforcement, and use of the codes.

This conference is held about every three years and is open to all those interested in attending as it is intended to enhance or supplement the training efforts currently offered by the organizations and DHCD.

DHCD & ICC preferred provider continuing education hours will be available and may also be recognized for credit by other industry organizations.
