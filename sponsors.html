Sponsors & Exhibitors

Our Top Sponsors & Exhibitors

The conference is hosted by members of the six statewide code expert organizations in Virginia. The training and networking opportunities provided will increase the knowledge of attendees in a variety of areas. We are anticipating 300 or more attendees in the areas of engineers, architects, contractors, permit technicians, building officials, fire professionals, code enforcement inspectors and others who are interested in codes and code development.

Become a Sponsor

As a sponsor, you will have a great opportunity to advertise and bring awareness to your organization directly to those in attendance. VCEC is offering the following sponsorship opportunities.

The Three Levels of Sponsorship Include:

  • Bronze Level - $750
  • Silver Level - $1500
  • Gold Level - $2500

Become an Exhibitor

Conference attendees are always eager to see displays of products and services that enhance their skills and knowledge. Therefore we are inviting you to be a part of this conference. As an exhibitor you will have the opportunity to display your products or services and network with attendees.

Exhibitor Fee is $750.00 which includes:

  • Logo and name on our website
  • Logo and name on conference program
  • Registration for two attendees*
  • Table
  • 2 Chairs
  • Power for electricity
  • Meals that are provided by conference

*Additional attendees will be an additional fee of $350.00
